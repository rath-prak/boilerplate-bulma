Choose font for digital UI, font for Marketing and print

The primary typeface chosen is Source Sans Pro and can be used for both print and digital.

Source Sans Pro is an open source type family designed by Paul D. Hunt. It is classified as sans serif and it was developed to work well both for print and screen usage.

Source Sans Pro also supports a wide variety of languages using Latin scrip as well as a full range of Māori macrons (tohutō).
